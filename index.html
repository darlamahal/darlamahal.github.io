About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Darla Mahal, and I am beginning my third year studying landscape architecture at Arizona State University. My expected graduation date is Spring 2027.

I chose to study landscape architecture because of my love for the environment and my desire to create sustainable, beautiful outdoor spaces. I love designing relaxing and tranquil outdoor spaces that harmonize with nature. One day I hope to create therapeutic landscapes that promote healing and well-being.
